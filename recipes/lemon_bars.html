Lemon Bars

Description

Lemon bar recipe. Perfect for condolences.

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 10 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar plus 2 tbsp, divided
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Filling:

  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Instructions

For the Crust:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and lightly grease a 9×9 inch pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, ½ cup powdered sugar, and salt. Cut the butter into the mixture until crumbly and then press into the bottom of the pan.
  3. Bake for 15-18 minutes until lightly golden. Meanwhile, make the filling.

For the Filling:

  1. In another mixing bowl, whisk all the filling ingredients together until combined.
  2. Pour over the cooked crust and place back in the oven for 17-20 minutes until filling is set.
  3. Cool completely, dust with remaining powdered sugar, and cut into squares.
